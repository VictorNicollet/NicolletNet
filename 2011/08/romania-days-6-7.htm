[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="640" caption="Sun + Rain + Forest"][/caption] Back in Târgu Jiu, we visited the scluptural ensemble by Constantin Brâncu? dedicated to the solders who died in the Great War. It is composed of the Table of Silence (twelve stone chairs around a large round stone table) followed closely by the Gate of the Kiss (a large stone gate), then the Path of Heroes (an empty path, about 1300 m long) and finally the Column of the Infinite. The artist designed the Path of Heroes so that a visitor sitting at the Table of Silence and looking through the Gate of the Kiss would see the Column of the Infinite in the distance. This was planned in 1935-1938. However, in 1937, king Carol II authorized the building of an orthodox church in the middle of the Path of Heroes, completely breaking the perspective.
